Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gorilla Tagging Team ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you play or access Gorilla Tagging, a multiplayer virtual reality game.

Gorilla Tagging uses online services including Microsoft PlayFab and Photon to provide account, multiplayer, networking, moderation, and voice features. By using the game, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Interpretation and Definitions

  • Application means the Gorilla Tagging VR game.
  • Account means a player account, PlayFab account record, platform account identifier, or other profile used to access the game.
  • Device means the VR headset, computer, console, mobile device, or other hardware used to access the game.
  • Personal Data means information that identifies, relates to, or can reasonably be linked to a person.
  • Service means the Application, website, multiplayer servers, moderation, and related online features.
  • Service Provider means a company that processes information for us, such as PlayFab, Photon, platform stores, hosting providers, analytics tools, or moderation tools.
  • Usage Data means technical, gameplay, diagnostic, security, and network data generated when you use the Service.

Information We Collect

Account and Player Information

We may collect player identifiers such as PlayFab ID, platform user ID, display name, username, avatar/cosmetic selections, friends or room identifiers, inventory, progress, currency, purchases, ban status, reports, and moderation notes.

Multiplayer and Gameplay Data

Because Gorilla Tagging is a multiplayer VR game, we may collect or process data needed to make online play work, including room codes, lobby membership, matchmaking information, game mode, position, movement, hand/controller actions, head direction, interactions, match results, leaderboards, anti-cheat signals, connection events, and crash/debug logs.

Device, Network, and Usage Data

We may automatically collect device type, operating system, app version, IP address, approximate region based on network routing, session times, server latency, errors, diagnostic data, and similar technical information.

Microphone and Voice Chat

Gorilla Tagging may request microphone permission so players can talk in multiplayer. If voice chat is enabled, your microphone audio may be captured by your device and transmitted in real time to other players in your room through Photon or another voice provider.

We do not use microphone access for advertising. We do not intentionally record or store voice chat unless a future safety, reporting, moderation, or platform feature clearly tells you that recording is occurring or unless required by law. Other players may still hear what you say, and you should not share private personal information in voice chat.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, update, and improve Gorilla Tagging.
  • To create and manage accounts, player profiles, inventory, cosmetics, progress, and game settings.
  • To provide multiplayer rooms, matchmaking, networking, latency routing, voice chat, and online gameplay.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to cheating, spam, harassment, abuse, fraud, security incidents, and rule violations.
  • To review player reports and moderation issues.
  • To analyze crashes, bugs, balancing, performance, and usage trends.
  • To comply with legal obligations and platform requirements.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party service providers to run parts of the game. These providers may process player and technical data for us according to their own terms and privacy documents.

  • Microsoft PlayFab may be used for backend game services, accounts, player data, inventory, statistics, analytics, moderation, cloud scripts, and live operations.
  • Photon / Exit Games may be used for multiplayer networking, matchmaking, real-time room communication, and voice chat.
  • Platform providers, such as VR app stores or payment providers, may process purchases, entitlements, device permissions, crash data, and platform account information.

You should also review the privacy policies and terms for the platform you use to download or play the game.

Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Service, with platform providers when necessary, with other players as part of normal multiplayer gameplay, in connection with safety/moderation, during a business transfer, or when required by law.

Other players may see or hear your display name, avatar, cosmetics, movement, gameplay actions, room presence, leaderboard placement, and voice chat if enabled.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including providing accounts, maintaining progress, enforcing rules, securing the game, complying with legal requirements, and keeping reasonable business records.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can disable microphone permission through your device or platform settings, but voice chat may stop working.
  • You can avoid sharing personal information in usernames, voice chat, room names, and player reports.
  • You may request access, deletion, or correction of certain personal information by contacting us.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under laws such as GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, or similar privacy laws.

Children’s Privacy

Gorilla Tagging is not intended for children under 13 unless allowed by the platform and applicable law with required parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent. If you believe a child provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information. However, no online game, server, or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Transfers

Game data may be processed in the United States or other countries where we, our service providers, or platform providers operate. Those countries may have privacy laws different from your country.

Do Not Track

Our website and game do not currently respond to browser Do Not Track signals.

Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to websites or services not operated by us. We are not responsible for third-party content, privacy policies, or practices.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page with the updated date.
